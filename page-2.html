Anime has a variety of unique, bizzare and funny characters. There are a range of different types of characters. For example, some characters are fighters, some are magicians and some are villians. There are some types of characters that are exclusive to that anime franchise, like the Saiyans in Goku.

A couple of famous characters are Naruto, Pikachu and Goku. Naruto can summon ninja toads that help him in battles. Goku is a Saiyan, an endangered warrior humaniod race, who has the ability to become a Sper Saiyan, which grants him increased strenght, endurance, etc. Pikachu is one of the Pokemmons, who was inspired by the Pika. He has the ability to send bolts of lightning from his tail zap his enimies. Like other Pokemons he too will evolve into a stronger species; in his case he will Raichu.

Naruto and Goku are strong fighters and Pikachu is a friend of the main character.
